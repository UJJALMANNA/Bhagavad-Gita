Daily Gita Sloka

A new verse every day, with a simple meaning — start your day with the wisdom of the Gita.

"karmaṇy-evādhikāras te mā phaleṣu kadācana,
mā karma-phala-hetur bhūr mā te saṅgo 'stv akarmaṇi"

— Bhagavad Gita, Chapter 2, Verse 47

Meaning: You have the right to perform your actions, but never to the fruits of those actions. Do not let the results be your motive for acting, nor let attachment draw you toward inaction.
